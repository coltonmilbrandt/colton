About Me

I am a creative engineer and leader who thrives on the cutting edge. As former President and CFO of Rent Buddy Inc, I led a local and global software development team of fourteen to successfully fund and launch an ambitious full-service rental property management suite. With a robust background in Tech and Finance, I’m uniquely suited as a Blockchain Developer, enthusiastically coding DeFi and infatuated with the fundamentals of distributed ledgers. My years of experience pushing my companies forward and being the primary problem solver have honed my skills to be extremely effective, direct, and organized, yet I’m naturally amiable, team-oriented, and supportive. I love to see my team thrive first and foremost.
